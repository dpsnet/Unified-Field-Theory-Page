统一场理论研究 Unified Field Theory Research

探索数学与物理的交叉领域，构建统一场理论的基础框架 Exploring the intersection of mathematics and physics, building the foundation for unified field theory

从分形几何到统一场理论 From Fractal Geometry to Unified Field Theory

本研究通过整合分形几何、模形式理论、谱维流动等现代数学工具，探索物理学中统一场理论的基础框架。研究涵盖从基础数学理论到物理应用的多个层面，旨在为统一场理论的发展提供新的数学基础和方法。 This research explores the fundamental framework of unified field theory in physics by integrating modern mathematical tools such as fractal geometry, modular form theory, and spectral dimension flow. The research covers multiple levels from basic mathematical theory to physical applications, aiming to provide new mathematical foundations and methods for the development of unified field theory.

核心理论 Core Theory

本研究的核心理论框架，涵盖数学基础与物理应用 The core theoretical framework of this research, covering mathematical foundations and physical applications

分形几何理论 Fractal Geometry Theory

研究分形维数的基本理论与无理数表示基础，提出统一分形维数表达式，为描述时空的分形结构提供数学工具。 Studies the fundamental theory of fractal dimensions and the basis of irrational number representation, proposes unified fractal dimension expressions, and provides mathematical tools for describing the fractal structure of spacetime.

模形式理论 Modular Form Theory

利用权为1的模形式空间构造PTE解系，建立模形式与素数分布的联系，为统一场理论提供数论基础。 Constructs PTE solution systems using weight 1 modular form spaces, establishes connections between modular forms and prime number distribution, and provides number theory foundations for unified field theory.

谱维流动理论 Spectral Dimension Flow Theory

研究谱维随尺度变化的流动方程，建立时空几何的能标依赖性模型，为理解量子引力提供新的视角。 Studies flow equations of spectral dimension with scale changes, establishes energy-scale dependent models of spacetime geometry, and provides new perspectives for understanding quantum gravity.

PTE问题研究 PTE Problem Research

探索Prouhet-Tarry-Escott问题的分形特征与模形式表示，构造素数PTE解，揭示整数集合的深层结构。 Explores fractal characteristics and modular form representations of Prouhet-Tarry-Escott problems, constructs prime PTE solutions, and reveals deep structures of integer sets.

高维推广与几何表示 High-dimensional Generalization and Geometric Representation

将PTE问题推广到高维空间，研究高维PTE解的几何性质，为统一场理论中的高维时空模型提供数学基础。 Generalizes PTE problems to high-dimensional spaces, studies geometric properties of high-dimensional PTE solutions, and provides mathematical foundations for high-dimensional spacetime models in unified field theory.

优化算法设计 Optimization Algorithm Design

基于L-BFGS-B算法设计高效的PTE解搜索算法，结合并行计算技术，提高大规模问题的处理能力。 Designs efficient PTE solution search algorithms based on L-BFGS-B algorithm, combines parallel computing technology, and improves processing capabilities for large-scale problems.

研究成果 Research Achievements

本研究的主要贡献和成果 The main contributions and achievements of this research

建立了PTE问题与分形几何的深层联系 Established deep connections between PTE problems and fractal geometry

揭示了PTE解的几何表示和分形特征，证明了PTE解空间的豪斯多夫维数为2m - n，为理解PTE解的分布规律提供了新的视角。 Revealed geometric representations and fractal characteristics of PTE solutions, proved that the Hausdorff dimension of PTE solution space is 2m - n, providing a new perspective for understanding the distribution规律 of PTE solutions.

建立了模形式理论与PTE解生成函数的对应关系 Established correspondence between modular form theory and PTE solution generating functions

利用权为1的模形式构造PTE解系，证明了素数PTE解的存在性，为素数分布规律的研究提供了新的方法。 Constructed PTE solution systems using weight 1 modular forms, proved the existence of prime PTE solutions, providing a new method for studying prime number distribution patterns.

建立了谱维流动与PTE解尺度变化的模型 Established model of spectral dimension flow and PTE solution scale changes

描述了PTE解的能标依赖性，为理解时空几何的分形结构提供了数学基础，与量子引力中的谱维流动概念建立了联系。 Described energy-scale dependence of PTE solutions, provided mathematical foundation for understanding fractal structure of spacetime geometry, and established connections with the concept of spectral dimension flow in quantum gravity.

设计了基于L-BFGS-B的高效PTE解搜索算法 Designed efficient PTE solution search algorithm based on L-BFGS-B

结合分形几何和模形式理论，提高了PTE解的搜索效率，为大规模PTE问题的研究提供了技术支持。 Combined fractal geometry and modular form theory, improved search efficiency for PTE solutions, providing technical support for research on large-scale PTE problems.

证明了素数PTE解的存在性和无限多解的存在性 Proved existence of prime PTE solutions and infinite solutions

利用模形式的傅里叶系数构造素数PTE解，分析了素数PTE解的分布规律，为素数研究领域提供了新的成果。 Constructed prime PTE solutions using Fourier coefficients of modular forms, analyzed distribution patterns of prime PTE solutions, providing new achievements in prime number research.

将PTE问题推广到高维空间 Generalized PTE problems to high-dimensional spaces

研究了高维PTE解的几何性质和构造方法，为统一场理论中的高维时空模型提供了数学基础。 Studied geometric properties and construction methods of high-dimensional PTE solutions, providing mathematical foundation for high-dimensional spacetime models in unified field theory.
